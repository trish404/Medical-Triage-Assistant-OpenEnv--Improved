Clinical Protocols

ESI v4
Emergency Severity Index
5-level triage scale developed by ACEP and ENA. Agents classify patients 1 (immediate life threat) to 5 (non-urgent) by evaluating chief complaint, vital signs, and anticipated resource needs.
OLDCARTS
Structured Intake Interview
Onset · Location · Duration · Character · Aggravating · Relieving · Timing · Severity. Standard nursing intake framework — agents must collect all 9 fields efficiently before completing intake.
START MCI
Mass Casualty Triage
Simple Triage and Rapid Treatment protocol used at mass casualty incidents. 5-step decision tree: walk → breathe → rate → pulse → commands → tag RED / YELLOW / GREEN / BLACK. Confusing RED and BLACK incurs a −0.15 penalty per error.
qSOFA
Sepsis Screening
quick Sequential Organ Failure Assessment. Score 1 point each for RR ≥ 22, GCS < 15, SBP ≤ 100. Score ≥ 2 = high risk → agents must flag the patient and order all 7 sepsis bundle items.
SBAR
Shift Handoff Protocol
Situation · Background · Assessment · Recommendation. Standardised nurse-to-nurse communication framework. Agents report 12 structured fields across 3 critical patients — incomplete handoffs risk patient safety and lower the score.
Capacity Law
Consent Assessment
4-criterion legal standard: understands information · appreciates situation · reasons through options · communicates choice. Agents assess capacity and navigate 6 consent pathways across 3 distinct scenarios. Dangerous shortcuts are penalised −0.30.
